What are Brain Tumors?

According to mayoclinic.org, "a brain tumor is a growth of cells in the brain or near it." When cells in a specific place in the brain start to grow, it can affect other parts of the brain in negative ways. Some brain tumors can in fact lead to death!

Common symptoms of brain tumors are: headaches, seizures, difficulty thinking, personality changes, weakness, loss of hearing, and changing in vision. This makes it important to identify a brain tumor as early as possible.

The most common brain tumors are: Gliomas and related brain tumors, choroid plexus tumors, embryonal tumors, and nerve tumors.

Uses and Backend Processes

Now how can this program be beneficial? This program can make parsing through images and identifying tumors more efficient. While combining together two of the biggest industries, this application utilizes machine learning methods to improve the medical industry.

This application uses YOLOv5 to identify the brain tumors, OpenCV to manage files and convert images to specific types, and Flask to manage the backend.
