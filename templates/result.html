How to read this image

What you see in front of you is an image of the MRI scan that you have supplied to this application. You will also see a red box around the tumor that was hopefully detected. If you do not see this box, the tumor was not detected. This probably means

1. The tumor does not exist
2. The tumor is too small to be detected.

Above the red box, you should see a number. This number is the "confidence" of this application. This means that the tumor was detected with this level of confidence. If you believe the confidence is too low (below 0.3), then the detected tumor might not be a tumor at all!

Good luck with your future detections!
